# Shajgoj Limited — Leave Policy

> Extracted from the Code of Conduct document

---

## Overview

Leave policies at Shajgoj Limited are available for eligible employees after successful completion of the **3-month probationary period**. All leave accrues and is calculated on a **calendar year basis** (January 1st to December 31st), except maternity and paternity leave.

**Important:** Different types of leave shall not be taken consecutively.

---

## Leave Types

### 1. Sick Leave

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Days per year | 14 days |
| Eligibility | After probation |
| Carry over | Not allowed (forfeited yearly) |

**Rules:**
- Provided when an employee falls unwell or is physically unable to attend office
- Sick leave for more than **3 consecutive days** must be supported by a certificate from a registered physician
- Medical certificate must be submitted within **one week** of absence
- Certificate must show diagnosis and duration of absence

---

### 2. Casual Leave

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Days per year | 10 days |
| Eligibility | After probation |
| Carry over | Not allowed (forfeited yearly) |

**Rules:**
- Maximum **3 days** at a time (5 days in exceptional circumstances)
- For educational/exam purposes: submit relevant documents and apply **15 days prior** to exam date
- Cannot be accumulated or carried over

---

### 3. Marriage Leave

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Days | 5 days |
| Eligibility | Confirmed employees |
| Lifetime limit | Once in entire employment |

**Rules:**
- Available at the time of employee's own marriage ceremony
- Weekends and government holidays are **not included** within the leave period
- Example: If taking 3 days before and 2 days after a weekend, the weekend doesn't count

---

### 4. Maternity Leave (Female Employees)

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Duration | 4 calendar months |
| Eligibility | Confirmed female employees |
| Lifetime limit | Twice in entire employment |

**Rules:**
- Apply at least **30 days prior** to commencement
- Must include medical certificate with expected delivery week
- Miscarriage/medical termination: up to **4 calendar weeks** with relevant medical documents

---

### 5. Paternity Leave (Male Employees)

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Days | 14 days |
| Eligibility | Confirmed male employees |
| Lifetime limit | Twice in entire employment |

**Rules:**
- **7 days** can be taken before delivery
- **7 days** after delivery (must be consumed within 3 months of baby's birth)
- **Cannot** take all 14 days consecutively

---

### 6. Bereavement Leave

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Days | 3 days maximum |
| Eligibility | Confirmed employees |

**Allowed for:**
- Loss of family member, relative, or friend
- Attending burial, funeral, entombment, or cremation
- Immediate post-death matters (Salat, Puja, prayer)

**Not allowed for:**
- Programs 4+ days after the death date
- 40th day programs or death anniversaries
- Death-related official purposes

---

## Leave During Probation Period

- **No standard leave entitlement** during probation
- Emergency leave may be allowed with pay (case by case)
- Any leave taken will be adjusted from annual casual/sick leave after confirmation
- If separation occurs before confirmation, leave will be deducted from final payment

---

## Attendance & Late Policy

| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| Late 3 consecutive days | Counted as 1 full day absence |
| Late 5 separate days in a month | Counted as 1 full day absence |
| Consequence | Adjusted with leave OR deducted from salary |

**AWOL (Absent Without Official Leave):**
- 3 consecutive working days without notification → 1 month suspension
- Notified via text and personal email
- No pay or benefits during suspension
- Leads to termination if no justification provided

---

## Government Holidays

Employees are entitled to all government-declared public holidays as notified by the Government of Bangladesh.
